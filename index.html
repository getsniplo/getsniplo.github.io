Smart variables make your snippets dynamic.

Don't just paste static text. Sniplo can inject the current date, clipboard contents, or page URL directly into your expanded snippets.

  • {date} — Today's date in any format
  • {clipboard} — Whatever's on your clipboard
  • {url} — The current page's URL
  • {title} — The current page's title
